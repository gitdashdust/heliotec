Design System

Tokens, type, color and component patterns reverse-engineered from the live production site at heliotec.energy — colors and sizes below are the actual values shipping today, not approximations.

Color

Ink (near-black navy) dominates dark sections; lime is the sole high-saturation accent reserved for primary actions and key numbers; indigo is used sparingly for links/highlights.

Buttons

Primary/secondary pairing is the standard CTA pattern site-wide (e.g. "Try for free" + "Schedule demo"). Corners use an 8px radius (--radius-lg), not a full pill.

Stat blocks

Numbers are the hero — used to lead with proof (savings, clients, CO2 eliminated) rather than adjectives.

Capability cards

Uses the signature single-corner "cut" shape (large radius on one corner only, square on the rest) instead of uniform rounded corners.
